## 🌟 Overview
This project explores the intersection of **Medicine** and **Computer Science** through an analytical study of first aid awareness in Alexandria, Egypt. Inspired by the **CS50** methodology, this work aims to bridge the gap between emergency medical response and algorithmic thinking.

* **first_aid_logic.py:** A Python-based Decision Tree script that simulates emergency medical responses based on the research findings.

## 🚀 Key Insights (The "Logic" of First Aid)
By treating community behavior as a **Data System**, the research identified:
* **79.5% Success Rate:** High awareness in bleeding management.
* **The "Broken Link":** Only **47.4%** awareness of the national emergency hotline (123), representing a system failure regardless of individual skill.
* **Cognitive Debugging:** Identified a critical need to correct the "behavioral algorithm" for fainting cases (37.5% incorrect practices).
